Key Insights 

The top-selling product overall for the period under review is the Muffin which generated a total of $2,401 (19%) of the total revenue
•Drilling into individual years, we observed that the Muffin was the top performer in 2024 and 2nd top performer for 2025 and 2026
•Sandwich and Croissant are the least performing products overall in terms of revenue
•Muffin is the least performing product at the Downtown location

Overall Business Recommendation
Menu performance and Stock/Ingredients Inventory:
	Keep a closer eye on the inventory to improve the performance of Sandwiches, maximise the potential of Muffins and make ingredients available for all other products
	Review the pastry chef in charge of Muffins at the Downtown location

Customer Spending:
	Create a loyalty plan for the 28-37 age band and provide healthy variety for the 58-67 age band to potentially improve their spending

Location/Employee Performance:
	All locations are conveniently achieving above their target; increase sales target
	Considering a 1:1 employee-ratio is not effective; we suggest those performing below 50% are laid off and others trained up
	Study the operational structure of Downtown (the best performing location) and replicate across other locations
